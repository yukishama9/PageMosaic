Today, PageMosaic is officially open source and available to everyone.

## Built for the gap AI tools leave behind

Tools like **Google Stitch** and **Vercel v0** are amazing at generating beautiful static pages in seconds. But once the design is in your hands, the question becomes: *now what?*

You have a folder of HTML files. Changing the navbar on one page means editing every file manually. Adding a new language means duplicating the entire project. The generated CSS is a mystery.

PageMosaic was built exactly for this moment — the gap between *AI-generated output* and *a maintainable website*.

## Not just for developers

You don't need to be a designer. You don't need Figma.

If you're a **lightweight user** — a blogger, indie maker, or small business owner — who just needs a clean static website without the overhead of a design tool or a full framework, PageMosaic gives you visual control over your site's components, theme, and content in one simple interface.

And if you care about **open-source software** — tools that are transparent, community-driven, and free forever — PageMosaic is a small but genuine alternative to the heavyweights. No subscription. No lock-in. Pure HTML you own completely.
